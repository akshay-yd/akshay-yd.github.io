About the project

Stress is a state or a feeling of various psychological and physiological pressures experienced by people throughout their lives, hence it is important to keep the stress factor under control. It can be controlled by various mechanisms. This process is called Stress Management.

This test determines how stressed a person is, which is done based on the person's behaviour and habits. This information is captured by a questionnaire which will be provided to the person. Depending on the answers, the system classifies the stress level of person into low, medium or high accordingly.
